# Processing Notes — High-Capacity RDHEI Edge-Directed Prediction & Entropy Coding (Qiu 2025)

- **Reproduction tier:** B

## What was reproduced
Predictability-based high-capacity (~1 bpp) reversible embedding under strong encryption with exact recovery.

## Reproduced vs reported
Principle and rate reproduced; the **edge-directed predictor + adaptive entropy coder** are approximated by fixed MSB prediction (stated openly), so reproduced rate is a conservative baseline.
